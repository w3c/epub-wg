  • public-{{ site.wgname_lc }}-wg@w3.org: the group’s primary mailing list. Group members are automatically signed up. The archives are publicly visible, and anyone can post a mail.
  • member-{{ site.wgname_lc }}-wg@w3.org: the group’s member confidential mailing list. Group members are automatically signed up. The archives are visible to W3C members only, and only persons with a W3C user accounts can post a mail.
  • group-{{ site.wgname_lc }}-testing@w3.org: list dedicated to the testing activity of the Working Group. The archives are only publicly visible, any anyone can post a mail.
  • group-{{ site.wgname_lc }}-wg-chairs@w3.org: list for chairs and W3C staff contact(s). The archives are only visible to the chairs and the W3C staff, but anyone can post a mail.
